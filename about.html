About us

Who we are

The Modern European Philosophy Research and Translation Institute (MEPRTI) is an independent, impartial organisation with no religious or political affiliation.

Our goals and objectives

We aim to provide research and translation of modern and contemporary European philosophy works into English and Arabic and to offer impartial, objective, and independent introductions and explanatory observations to major thinkers who have had a critical intellectual influence since the XVI century.

How we are funded

We rely on funding from donors. Please contact us if you wish to support us.
