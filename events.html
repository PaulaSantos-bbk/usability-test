Poetry and Philosophy Conference

The University of Warwick will host this BSA Synergy Conference on 16-17 March 2020 focusing on questions of shared interest for poetry and philosophy. Poets, philosophers, philosopher-poets, poet-philosophers, and poetry scholars will bring their expertise and experience to the conversation.
